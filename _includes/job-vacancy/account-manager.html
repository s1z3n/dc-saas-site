Account Manager

(Sales & Marketing)

Education / Experience

  • Minimum 2 years of Account Management and Marketing experience.
  • The successful candidate must possess at least a Bachelor's Degree in Finance, Economics, Business Administration, Marketing, Computer Science, Management Information System, Mathematics, Physics & Engineering, or related degrees.

Summary of Position

  • Ensure an effective and efficient first point of contact for clients with company.
  • To identify and create leads for all business development prospects besides maintaining a close professional relationship with influential parties concerned.
  • To build a reliable and comprehensive data base of prospects and customers for the Company.
  • Maintain company's professional image through all interactions with clients and ensure that clients' satisfaction is realized.
  • To execute the necessary Marketing programs and activities in the total business development and Sales process.
  • Involve directly and/or indirectly in enhancing the system to meet new markets' and/or clients' requirements.
  • Keep abreast and improve skills and knowledge in both technology and business requirements.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Excellent presentation skills including both development and delivery.
  • Ability to excel in ambiguous technical and business situations by adapting plans and approach; analytical and organizational skills, negotiation, judgment and decision-making skills.
  • Excellent interpersonal skills to build and maintain positive working relationships across groups and all management levels.
  • Outstanding time-management skills and a demonstrated ability to work independently as well as in a team environment to meet deadlines while juggling many projects and priorities simultaneously.
  • Results oriented, energetic and focused with a strong desire to learn and succeed.
  • Develop and maintain a deep understanding of your client base.
  • Build and maintain relationships with a list of dedicated clients and provide ongoing strategic consultation and support.
